About Vivo Cleaning

Your trusted cleaning partner in Adelaide

Vivo Cleaning is a locally owned and operated cleaning business based in Felixstow, South Australia. For over 10 years, we've been delivering professional cleaning solutions for homes, offices, schools, and community spaces across Adelaide and surrounding suburbs.

Our team of fully trained, police-cleared cleaners are passionate about delivering consistent, reliable results — using eco-friendly, hospital-grade products that are safe for your family, your clients, and the environment.
